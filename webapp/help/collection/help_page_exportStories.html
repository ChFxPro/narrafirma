Export stories or story forms

You can use this NarraFirma page to export your stories or story forms to CSV files for use outside NarraFirma.

What to do here

Choose a story collection, then click "Export story form" or "Export story collection."

Frequently-asked questions
When I try to read my stories (or story form) in Excel, it doesn't look right.

Excel doesn't convert CSV files to its format very well when you open them directly. To open a CSV file correctly in Excel, create an empty spreadsheet, then choose Data - Get External Data - Import Text File. When asked what delimiter to use (to distinguish one column from the next), choose "comma." You may need to try a few different options for the "Text qualifier" and "Text encoding."

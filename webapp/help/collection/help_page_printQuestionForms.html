Print story forms

This page is for printing story forms to be handed out to participants. If you use this option, you will probably use your completed story forms to enter stories into NarraFirma.

What to do here

Choose a story form to print, then click the Print Story Form button. The form will appear in a separate window, and you can print it from your browser.

Frequently-asked questions
Can I format my story form before I print it?

Yes, in two ways. First, you can enter custom CSS on the "Design, generate, or import story forms" page to format the printed story form. Second, you can copy the story form from your browser and paste it into your word processor, then format it there.

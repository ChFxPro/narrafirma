This NarraFirma page lists the collection pages of your project and shows the reminders you wrote about them.

The collection pages are:

  • Choose collection methods
  • Plan story collection sessions
  • Write story eliciting questions
  • Write questions about stories
  • Write questions about participants
  • Design story forms
  • Start story collection
  • Print story forms
  • Enter stories
  • Review incoming stories
  • Spot check graphs
  • Stop story collection
  • Enter story collection session records

Frequently-asked questions
What kinds of story collection does NarraFirma support?

You can collect stories over the internet, or you can collect them in any other way and enter them yourself (as if you were responding over the internet).

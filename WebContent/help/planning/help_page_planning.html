Frequently-asked questions
Is the planning phase required?

The planning phase is placed outside the essential PNI triangle, so it's an optional activity. But in NarraFirma, the part of the planning phase where you describe your participant groups and describe your resources drive the recommendations shown on these pages:

  • story collection venues
  • story eliciting questions
  • questions about stories
  • questions about participants
  • story collection sessions
  • sensemaking sessions
So if you want to get recommendations on any of these things later on, you should at least describe your participant groups and your resources in the planning phase.

Where can I enter a pilot story collection as part of planning?

Set it up in the Collection phase. Create a questionnaire, and use it online or enter your data by hand. You can even create a pilot catalysis report, then use it in your planning. NarraFirma doesn't have a special "reflect on your pilot story collection" page, but you can incorporate what you learned when you finalize your answers to the PNI planning questions.

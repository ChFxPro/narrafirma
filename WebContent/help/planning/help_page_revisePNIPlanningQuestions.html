Planning: Revise PNI Planning Questions
Why this page matters
Your answers to the PNI planning questions constitute a sort of agreement, with your team or just with yourself, on what your project is about (and what it is not about). You can use these answers as a touchstone to remind yourself what you are doing and why. You can use them to communicate the intent behind the project with people outside it.
What to do on this page
You can copy your draft answers (entered on the Answer PNI planning questions page) and revise them in the light of the planning activities you did:
  • Describe participant groups
  • About you
  • Tell project stories
  • Create project story elements
  • Assess story sharing

These will be your final answers, so make sure you are happy with them before you move on to the next phase of your project.

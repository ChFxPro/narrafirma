Planning: Describe participant groups
How to complete this page
Think about groups of people whose perspectives might differ in your project. They might see things differently, or have different amounts of power in the community or organization. If you need to consider groups separately, enter them here.
Why this page matters
The descriptions you enter here will create the recommendations you see on several other pages (story collection venues, story eliciting questions, questions about stories, questions about participants, story collection sessions, and sensemaking sessions). By thinking carefully about your participant groups now, you will lay the ground for solid decisions later in the project.
Questions you might have
What if I only have one group?
Just describe that one group. It's normal to have only one group if your project is simple.
What if I can't answer some of the questions?
Choose the answer "unknown." (Or do some more preliminary work until you can answer the question.)
